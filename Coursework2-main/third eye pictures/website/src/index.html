ABOUT COMPANY

Third Eye Pictures is a creative, forward-thinking and realist filmmaking production company founded in August 2020. The team are a perfect mix of individual, specialist skill sets of everything from idea brainstorming and script writing to editing and festival distribution. The company is driven by uncovering unseen perspectives around life and are passionate about telling the stories of unheard and silenced characters within society. Third Eye Pictures is on a mission to make film more accessible to those up and coming in the industry and to break down the boundaries of being told you can't.
